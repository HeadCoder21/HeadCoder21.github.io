About Us &
Our Mission
Nowadays, geography is becoming a lost skill. Some people can't even look at a map and point to where they live. It's super important for us to learn where cities and towns are around the world and to identify them at a glance. This coder was motivated to create this website after some- one said that continental France was in South America. Seriously. If someone gives you a map of the world and asks you to point to New York City, Chicago, or Los Angeles, you should already know where these places are. It's our mission to reteach this lost knowledge and bring geography back to the stage.

So, how does the game work? Well, we'll show you a picture of a city, and you'll have to tell us its name, which country it's in, and what continent it's on. At the end, you'll see your final score! We've got easy, medium, and hard quizzes, and if you need to look at a map beforehand, select "View the Map" in the top bar. Are you ready? Click one of the buttons below!
